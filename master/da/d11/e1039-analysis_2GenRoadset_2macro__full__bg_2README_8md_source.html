1 # Macro to generate BG events from the full-BG simulation
2 
3 This directory is to generate BG events from the full-BG simulation.
4 It outputs a small ROOT file named `bg_tree.root`, which will be used later in `macro_ana`.
5 
6 
7 ## Setting of Event Generation
8 
9 The setting of the event generation is configured in `Fun4All.C` as usual.
10 The SubsysReco module that creates `bg_tree.root` is `src/SubsysRecoBG.(h|cc)`.
11 You can modify them as you need.
12 
13 The configuration of `SQPileupGen` was taken from `abinashpun/e1039-abi-projects/PileupDev` on 2021-12-21.
14 `e906_rf00.root` was copied from it also.
