constexpr double AnaBG::N_RF_PER_SPILL = 186e6
staticprotected

N of RFs per spill.

Expected counts per spill = [N of "fired" events] / [N of all events] * [N of filled RFs] N of filled RFs = 186e6 = 588 * 369000 * 6 / 7
